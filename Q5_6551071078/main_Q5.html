Healthy Eating

Healthy eating provides your body with essential nutrients, maintains energy, and helps prevent
diseases. Prioritize vegetables, fruits, and whole grains while limiting foods high in fat and sugar.
Combine this with drinking enough water and regular exercise to live a balanced and healthy life.
